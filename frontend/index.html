DeScroll

Remove distractions. Reclaim your focus.

Download DeScroll

DeScroll is a lightweight browser extension that blocks YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels on the web.

It removes endless scrolling feeds so you can stay focused on what matters.

Everything runs locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

Features

  • Hides YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels from your feed
  • Runs entirely in your browser with no tracking or data collection
  • Free and open-source with no hidden costs or subscriptions

How to Install DeScroll

  1. Download the DeScroll ZIP file
  2. Unzip the downloaded file
  3. Open chrome://extensions in your browser
  4. Enable Developer Mode (top-right corner)
  5. Click Load unpacked
  6. Select the unzipped DeScroll folder
Works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers.
